Paradux Comics is a joke-a-day webcomic I started with my girlfriend Laurie. Originally it was a way for the two of us to collaborate, I in writing and she in drawing, but it grew into a project with enough integrity to stand on it's own.

The stips are mostly one panel, drawing inspiration from Calvin & Hobbes and The Far Side among others. The comic is currently on hiatus, as Laurie is studying CGI for a year in Florida!

Crooks and Nannies is a sketch comedy and film production trifecta comprising myself, Dave Beede and Andy McCarthy. We started in 2004 in college, making short films and working on the campus sketch comedy show. We're now working on a feature, The Night The Ants Came!

Check out some of our sketches at http://crooksandnannies.com, or click the Video tab.

A recently unearthed Crooks and Nannies sketch, The Man From The Future was originally shot in college in 2008.

Also, check out that transparent green background! Looks like nuclear waste!

More sketches are coming soon (from this decade, even!).
